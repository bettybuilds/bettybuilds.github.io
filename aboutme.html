About me

Introduction

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Betti, and I am a data analyst with a passion for turning complex data into actionable insights. I have experience in reporting & controlling, business intelligence, and data analysis, and I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges.

My educational background includes completing the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, a Data Science Bootcamp at Pragmatic Institute, and Python courses at the University of Michigan. I also have a Bachelor's degree in Business Management and Administration from Budapest Business University. I chose Enterprise Management specialization which includes studies in Decision Making Softwares, Controlling, Accounting, Statistics & Probability Studies, Project Management, Marketing, and Economics.

During my time at University, I had the opportunity to advise a real-world project where I suggested marketing strategies for a Hungarian program looking to expand its manager training program ('A jövő vezetői'). This experience helped me develop analytical and communication skills, as I had the chance to go through every step from market research to the final strategy presentation.

As a professional with a finance and IT background, I have experience in owning financials for B2B clients in the oil industry. Working collaboratively with multinational divisions, I strive to deliver results and ensure customer satisfaction.

In addition to my finance expertise, I am an innovative thinker with experience in presenting ideas to management. I was invited to a DevOps meeting to share my idea for a new Management Reporting System and supported its implementation. My strength lies in identifying business requirements and translating them into functional requirements and use cases.

I am self-motivated and proactive, having trained myself to use outdated online training material to become the key person for Jedox Planning and Performance Management software in an IT startup environment. This allowed me to create dashboards, user manuals, ETLs and ELTs, scripts with SQL and occasionally Groovy.

As an analyst with a passion for problem-solving and a strong foundation in statistics, I am excited to apply my skills to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets. With experience in reporting and visualization, I have developed a keen eye for spotting patterns and trends in data, and I am skilled in using tools like SQL, Python, and Excel to clean, manipulate and visualize data.

I am committed to delivering high-quality work and collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive results.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
