About me

Introduction

Hey, I’m Betti - a data analyst with a strong focus on business intelligence and someone who’s genuinely passionate about solving problems, spotting patterns and telling meaningful stories with data.

I work best in fast-paced, agile environments where I can dig in, learn quickly, and make an impact. I’ve built my career around being a self-starter. I’m someone who doesn’t wait around for perfect documentation or detailed walkthroughs. If there’s a messy dataset, a broken dashboard or a reporting process that’s wasting time, I’ll figure it out, optimize it and get it running better than before.

Right now I’m a Business Planning & Reporting Senior Associate @ Cognizant where I lead end-to-end Power BI projects across multiple departments. I work directly with stakeholders (often at Director level and up) to translate business needs into clean, scalable BI solutions. Whether it’s optimizing legacy reports, building advanced data models or training teams post-deployment, I’m all about delivering results that are easy to use and built to last. I’ve also introduced Python automations, revived forgotten projects and designed tools that have saved hours of manual work across teams.

In December 2025, I’ll step into a BI Lead role helping shape the strategic direction of our reporting infrastructure.

I’ve also worked at startups where I owned everything BI-related, from defining KPIs and cleaning raw data to building predictive models and presenting insights to senior leaders. I love working with cross-functional teams and I thrive in roles where I can take full ownership of a challenge whether it’s in marketing, sales, finance or operations.

Some of my favorite tools include Power BI, SQL, Python, Excel and Power Query but I’m always up for learning new ones when the job calls for it. I’ve earned certificates from Microsoft, Google and Pragmatic Institute and I come from a background in business, finance, and tech. This wide range of experience helps me connect the dots between the data and the bigger picture.

At the end of the day I’m here to get things done. I don't just build reports. I uncover the story in the data, find anomalies and deliver insights that actually help people make better decisions.

Thanks for stopping by and feel free to connect anytime!
