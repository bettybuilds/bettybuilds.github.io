About me

Introduction

I am a data-driven individual with a passion for analytics and reporting. My educational background includes a Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, a Data Science Bootcamp from Pragmatic Institute, and Python courses from the University of Michigan. In addition, I hold a degree in Business Management and Administration from Budapest Business University.

My professional experience includes working as a Reporting and Business Intelligence Specialist at NGENA, where I develop reporting requirements, maintain documentation, participate in IT projects, and train colleagues. I'm working as a key person regarding the usage of Jedox Planning and Reporting Management System, while solving issues, eliminating bugs, managing administrative functions, implementing reports, automating workflows, writing scripts in mySQL/Groovy and writing User Manual.I also worked as an ARC Analyst at BP, where I managed and reviewed month-end closing processes, gathered and standardized data streams from various functions for forecasting activities. Besides that I was working closely with developers on software related issues and upgrades by building up ideas on how to automate recurring tasks and performing the testing before final approval.

I am proficient in Python, Pandas, NumPy, mySQL, Git, VBA, SAP, Analysis for Office, BeX Analyzer and Jedox. I have a keen eye for detail, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work well in a team environment.
