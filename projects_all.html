Cyclistic - The bike sharing company

A Google case study analysis using mySQL for merging historical data, then using Python to clean and manipulate data finishing with fact-finding visualization.

The marketing analyst team wants to understand how casual riders and annual members use Cyclistic bikes differently in Chicago. Rather than creating a marketing campaign that targets all-new customers, there might be a very good chance to convert casual riders into members.
